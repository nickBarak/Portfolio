      Several locations on the UC Santa Barbara campus and in Isla Vista have closed due to poor air quality as a result of the Thomas Fire burning in Ventura County, while others are operating with limited services.

      The map below shows locations in I.V. and on campus that are closed Friday or providing limited services.

      The following departments will be closed Friday:

      • Academic Initiatives
      • Visitors Center
      • A.S. Bike Shop
      • A.S. Food Bank
      • A.S. Program Board
      • Campus Learning Assistance Services (CLAS)
      • Career Services
      • Children’s Centers
      • College of Engineering – Undergraduate Office
      • Disabled Students Program (DSP)
      • Early Academic Outreach Program (EAOP)
      • Educational Opportunity Program (EOP)
      • First-Year and Graduate Student Initiatives
      • Office of Financial Aid and Scholarships
      • Gevirtz Graduate School of Education (GGSE)

      • Office of Judicial Affairs (OJA)
      • MultiCultural Center (MCC)

      • Student Affairs Grants and Development
      • Student Information Systems and Technology (SIS&T)
